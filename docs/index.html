CHECKING TOOLS

  • Have I Been Pwned - To check if your email or phone is in a data breach.
  • Firefox Monitor - Also to check if your email or phone is in a data breach, Firefox use Have I Been Pwned data.
  • Periksa Data - Also to check if your email or phone is in a data breach, but in Indonesian.
  • Have I Been Pwned: Pwned Passwords - To check if your password is part of a data breach.
  • Passfault - To check how strong your password is.
  • Mozilla Observatory - To scan your website security and how to configure your sites safely and securely.
  • Exodus - To check the privacy audit of your Andoird apps.
  • VirusTotal - To analyze suspicious files and URLs to detect types of malware.
  • Blacklight-The Markup - To inspect website privacy in real-time.
  • Jeffrey's Image Metadata Viewer - To check pictures' metadata.
